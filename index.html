Hello there!

I am a 20 year old photographer, videographer, and designer from Klein Texas! Graphic design, typography, marketing, photography, and documenting events in digital media have been passions of mine since I was very young. I started my photography journey by taking photos of rescue dogs for their online adoption profiles in middle school, and then joined the yearbook staff at my high school. In yearbook, everyone worked together as a team and I realized that I need that collaborative environment. I loved constantly learning new skills and information with my peers at such a rapid pace. I am currently a junior in college at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln, and working as a Creative Media Student Assistant for the Huskers athletic department. The staff and leadership in the Corn Crib is unmatched, and I have learned so much already in just my first year and a half. One of the most important things to me is showing the athletes as people with stories and not just players. I began freelancing in 2019, and love working with clients to immortalize your memories! Ultimately, I just love the art of capturing a story, and I am ecstatic to have the opportunity to continue to study my passion and make an impact on the world through it.
